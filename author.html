About the Author

Paulo Coelho

Born in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil, in 1947, Paulo Coelho lived a rebellious life as a hipster and a songwriter before becoming an international legend as an author. He is now one of the most widely read authors in the world because of his hit book “The Alchemist”.

His writing style is known for its simplicity but having deep spiritual themes. Coelho believes that everyone has a "Personal Legend", a destiny that they must fulfill in order to find true happiness.

Fun Fact: He wrote The Alchemist in only two weeks! He said the story was already written in his mind and soul, and he just had to put it on paper.

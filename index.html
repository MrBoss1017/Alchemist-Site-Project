About the Book

The Alchemist is a magnificent book by Paulo Coelho that tells the story of Santiago, a young shepherd boy who travels the desert to the Egyptian pyramids in search of a treasure he saw in a dream.

Along his journey, he meets an Alchemist who teaches him that "when you want something, all the universe conspires in helping you to achieve it." It is a story about the importance of listening to your heart and following your "Personal Legend."

Main Message:

The core message of the book is that the journey to find your treasure is more important than the treasure itself. It teaches that fear is a bigger obstacle than the obstacle itself, and that everything in the universe is connected by a "Universal Language."

The Synopsis

The Plot: A Masterpiece

Santiago’s journey is more than just traveling through the desert, it’s a lesson in "Alchemy", the art of turning lead into gold, meaning that lead is a normal life, and gold is your destiny.

The story follows a shepherd boy who learns that the journey to find his Personal Legend is more important than the treasure itself.

Plots & Characters

Santiago

A shepherd boy who learns to listen to his heart and read the omens of the universe.

The Alchemist

The wise mentor who helps Santiago understand the "Soul of the World" and speak the universal language.

Symbols

Urim and Thummim: Two stones that symbolize the ability to make decisions and read the signs of the universe.
